A student portal is an online gateway where students can log into the website to access important program information.

Student portal contain information on courses offered, transcripts, email programs, timetables, exam schedules and department contact numbers. This also offer links to useful Web resources, such as research tools and online journals.

Using the portal instead of printing information in booklets saves printing and paper costs. It also benefits the environment by using fewer resources. The portal makes it easier for students to access important information from anywhere at any time of day.
